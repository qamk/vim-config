# Success Criteria

A report is successful when:

| Audience | Success Indicator |
|----------|------------------|
| **Non-Technical** | They can confidently communicate the issue/resolution to customers |
| **Developing Engineers** | They can explore similar issues independently and understand the patterns |
| **Principal+ Engineers** | They can evaluate solution quality and make informed architectural decisions |
| **Mixed/Leadership** | They understand business impact and can allocate resources appropriately |
| **Tier 2 Support** | They can immediately continue investigation without repeating Tier 1 work |
| **Tier 3 Engineering** | They can implement fixes with full context and no need to re-investigate |
| **Post-Mortem (mixed)** | New team member understands what happened; leadership can assess impact; engineering can reproduce the investigation; next steps are trackable |
